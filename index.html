About us

Relocating your home or office, has always been a cumbersome deal. The trouble of packing all your stuff safely and precisely, loading it and then taking it to the new location is easier said than being done. What can be worse than getting trapped by movers who are going to turn your moving day into your worst nightmare? Unfulfilled commitments, hidden charges and pathetic support will leave you traumatized.

If you have already experienced moving and have experienced the worse side of it, you know what it takes. That’s the reason you are probably here. What you need is a professional moving service, who knows how to take care of its customers. No one does it better than Los Angeles Moving Center who knows what it takes to turn your experience into a great one. Residential or commercial moving, our team knows how to deliver you the very best. Professional attitude, finest tools and a cooperative team- that is all about Los Angeles Moving Center.

Services

Local moving

Los Angeles Moving Center handles all moves with the same effort and consideration whether it is to the next neighborhood or next state. It is our goal to make your move as easy as possible. We treat your belongings with the most professional care, for we use top quality packing materials, employ skilled moving associates, and transport with clean and well-equipped trucks. We want to provide you with the most complete and worry-free move. We will supply you with all the necessary tools to move your possessions quickly and easily.

Long Distance

Los Angeles Moving Center is ready to provide you with the most comprehensive, extra safe, and best priced long distance move. We understand the difficulties of relocating, especially long distance, and that is why we do everything in our power to make your moving experience as simple as possible. Customer satisfaction is our main concern and our employees will help you with any aspect of the relocation procedure from our first conversation to the time you are settled into your new home.

Warehousing & Storage

Storing your goods for a temporary period isn’t a service that you are always going to need. In most of the moves, there is a slight delay before you can finally relocate to a new location. During this period, you just can’t simply put your business or household items at the stake. Your items, worth thousands of dollars, are prone to damage or theft. The solution lies in storing these items at a safe place for the temporary period. Los Angeles Moving Center offers storage services to our clients, ensuring the safety of their items.

Full packing & unpacking

Packing and unpacking goods might seem like a simple job to do but it isn’t. We offer professional packing and unpacking service that ensures the safety of your goods. Organizing the whole packing procedure is imperative and our experts are experienced enough to take care of your needs. We understand that packing items can vary from job to job, and therefore provide customized packing solutions to meet your needs in the perfect manner. In addition to boxes, we offer specialized packing materials for ultra delicate, valuable or heavy items.
